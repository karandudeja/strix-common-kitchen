Order of cleaning

  • There are 12 rooms in this corridor; 1216, 1217, 1218, 1219, 1220, 1221, 1224, 1225, 1226, 1227, 1228 and 1229
  • The cleaning order proceeds in a cycle, from 1216 to 1229, incrementally. For example; after 1216, it will next be the turn of 1217.
  • After 1229, the cleaning card goes back to 1216

Frequency & Timing of the cleaning

  • If the math is correct, each person will get their cleaning duty, once every 9 to 12 weeks, i.e. after every 2.5 to 3 months.
  • The kitchen is cleaned individually, every week (Mon - Sun). We all take turns.
  • Cleaning is a shared responsibility of all corridor residents, but the duties of the cleaning person in-charge during the week, are done individually.
  • The cleaning-duty week starts on a Monday, and ends on a Sunday, of that week.
  • Each person has a week assigned to them, as per rotation of the SSSB cleaning card-sticker.
  • After the week is over, the cleaning card is transferred to the next person's shelf (or cabinet).
